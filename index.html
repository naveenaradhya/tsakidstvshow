Which character plays good soccer?





Lionel is the smartest in the Amaze Squad. He plays soccer and he is very kind and caring. He often helps other kids with math and science.

Abdul is the adventurous and brave kid in the Amaze Squad. Another great trait of his is that he is determined, he also is very good at basketball.

Robb is the sports kid in the group, he plays essentially all sports, but he particularly likes volleyball and baseball. He is the impulsive one in the group, but Jamaal helps him with patience.

Jamaal is the most calm person in the Amaze Squad. He keeps everyone calm (Especially Robb!) and he is very good at solving social/emotional problems.
