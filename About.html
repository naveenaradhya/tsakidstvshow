VYI TV began in Denver in 2020 as Colorado's public television station.
It is now Colorado's most loved television network, with stations nationwide!
VYI TV is committed to providing entertaining, age-appropriate and quality content to kids.
We value the public's trust and respect the high standards set for our work.
VYI TV strives for fairness, objectivity and responsiveness to the public's feedback and input.
And we ensure transparency in our news and information content, while avoiding any improper influence from funders or other outside sources.
